The After Bedtime Sessions

No tracks, no studios, just a song the way you would hear it if you were there.

A project that stemmed from a collaboration with the Nashville-based country music duo, Drew and Lacey. They explain the idea on their site like this: "The After Bedtime Sessions is about putting the kids to bed, gathering in the living room, and creating music. Each session will be different: a new mix of musicians, a brand new song, an old bluegrass standard that we just rediscovered, or a 10-minute long fiddle jam. No tracks, no studios, just a song the way you would hear it if you were there."

Designing in Browser

This project was an experiment in designing solely "in browser" to see what would happen. I did all the coding, design, and branding for this project. I definitely learned that at my current skill level I missed a few things by starting right "in-browser", and I am okay with that, but I found it an interesting way to begin a design, and thinking of ways to implement from the infancy of an idea.
